## 🚀 About Me  
I am an Agricultural Engineer and MSc candidate specializing in **Remote Sensing**, **Precision Agriculture**, and **Water Resources**.  
I integrate **satellite imagery**, **UAV thermal/multispectral data**, and **hydrological modeling** to optimize irrigation, improve crop performance, and support climate-smart agriculture.

🔬 My work focuses on:  
- 🛰️ Sentinel-2 & Landsat 8/9 Analysis  
- 🌡️ CWSI Modeling with UAV–Satellite Fusion  
- 💧 Evapotranspiration (METRIC, TSEB, AquaCrop, FAO-56)  
- 🤖 Machine Learning for Crop Yield  
- ☁️ Google Earth Engine (GEE)  
- 🛩️ Thermal & Multispectral Drone Workflows
